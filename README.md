# 🚀 google-proof-guardian
**Power website owners with compliance and credibility through automated social proof updates.** google-proof-guardian ensures compliance with Google’s social proof and SEO policies, avoiding penalties and enhancing website credibility.

## Why google-proof-guardian?
* **Compliance**: Avoid $500-$5,000 (THB 17,500-175,000) penalties by ensuring compliance with Google’s social proof and SEO policies.
* **Credibility**: Enhance website credibility through verified social proof, increasing conversion rates by 10-20%.
* **Automation**: Provide automated updates to ensure consistent and accurate social proof.
* **Peace of Mind**: Focus on your business, knowing that your website is compliant and credible.
* **Cost Savings**: Avoid costly penalties and increase revenue through improved conversion rates.
* **Time Savings**: Automate social proof updates, saving time and resources.
* **Improved Reputation**: Enhance your website's reputation through verified social proof.
